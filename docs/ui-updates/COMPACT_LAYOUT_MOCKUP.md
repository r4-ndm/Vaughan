## Responsive Design Notes

### On Small Screens (< 600px height)
- Scrolling enabled for send form when expanded
- Balance and quick actions remain at top
- Easy to collapse send form to return to compact view

### On Medium Screens (600-900px height)
- Most operations visible without scrolling
- Optimal user experience
- Advanced options may require minimal scroll

### On Large Screens (> 900px height)
- Everything visible without scrolling
- Even with advanced options expanded
- Premium experience
